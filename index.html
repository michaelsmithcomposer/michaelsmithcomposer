About
I'm a Sydney-based musician. In 2024 I graduated from the Sydney Conservatorium of Music with a degree in Composition for Creative Industries, and released my debut electronic EP Double Sheath. I'm interested in the intersection of music and technology. I programmed and built an experimental hardware synthesizer, and am currently developing a software audio manipulation tool.
